Security

Our approach to protecting the system and your data.

Overview

We prioritize the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our systems. Below are high-level practices we follow to keep ResQNow secure.

Technical Controls

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) for all web and API traffic.
  • Access controls and role-based permissions for internal tools.
  • Regular backups and monitored logging for incident response.

Operational Practices

  • Periodic vulnerability scanning and patching.
  • Minimal data access — we limit who can view personal data.
  • Incident response plan and escalation procedures.

Reporting Security Issues

If you find a security vulnerability, please email security@resqnow.example with details. We will acknowledge and respond promptly.
