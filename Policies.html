Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026 — How ResQNow collects, uses, and protects data.

Summary

ResQNow is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your choices about that data.

Data We Collect

  • Contact information (name, email, emergency contacts) you provide when registering or contacting support.
  • Location and SOS alert metadata when you trigger emergency alerts (timestamp, coordinates, device model).
  • Communications logs for SMS/WhatsApp/email confirmations (kept for troubleshooting).

How We Use Data

  • To deliver emergency notifications and coordinate assistance.
  • To investigate abuse, troubleshoot, and improve service reliability.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

You can update or delete certain account information via your profile. For data deletion requests, contact support at support@resqnow.example or use the contact form.

Data Retention

We retain SOS alert logs and related metadata for a limited time to support investigations and improve safety features. Retention durations depend on legal or operational needs.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@resqnow.example

More Details

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect data, including encryption in transit and access controls. Note that no system is perfectly secure.

We may use third-party providers for messaging and analytics — those services have their own privacy practices and contractual protections.

ResQNow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
